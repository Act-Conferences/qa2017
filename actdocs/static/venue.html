Rugby train station is a short taxi ride (5mins) to the venue. Taxis are available outside the station.

Parking

If you plan on travelling by car, please be aware that although Parking is available at the Clock Towers Shopping Centre, for a reduced rate for hotel guests. However, the car park is securely locked from 7pm until 7am, except on Sundays when it is locked from 4pm until 8am. Outside of these hours, the Asda pay & display car park is available

Public Transport

Plane

Rugby itself does not have an international airport, but flights to/from Birmingham International (BHX), London Heathrow (LHR) and Manchester International (MAN) are all accessible using the rail network.

Rugby's closest airport is Birmingham International, and is less than 30 minutes by train.

Train

The Rugby train station is located on the London Euston to Birmingham New Street line, and also includes Birmingham International.

Trains from London Euston, travelling North, and trains travelling South from Birmingham New Street and Birmingham International, run at least once an hour throughout the day and evening. The last train typically being around 00:34am from Euston, and 23:10 from Birmingham New Street. Tickets for a single journey are from £6.00, if paid in advance. You can book tickets in advance online via London Midland.
